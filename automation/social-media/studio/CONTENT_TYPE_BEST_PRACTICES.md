# Content Type Best Practices — AXKAN Content Studio

These are the best practices injected into every prompt generation, per content type.
The system uses these to ensure professional, viral-quality output regardless of topic.

---

## 1. CAROUSEL (Static IG Carousel)
**Format:** 4:5 vertical (1080x1350px) | 4-8 slides

**Slide 1 (Cover/Hook) — 80% of the battle:**
- Must stop the scroll in under 0.5 seconds
- Bold, high-contrast visuals with minimal text (under 8-10 words)
- Creates a curiosity gap — makes people NEED to swipe
- Striking colors, unexpected compositions, or dramatic close-ups

**Visual Consistency Across Slides:**
- Same color palette, typography style, and visual language on every slide
- Consistent background treatment throughout
- Same illustration/photography style and detail level
- Creates a cohesive swipe experience that feels like one story

**Each Slide Must Justify the Swipe:**
- Every slide reveals something new — angle, detail, variation, or context
- Progressive disclosure: each builds on the previous
- No two slides should look too similar

**Engagement-Optimized Design:**
- High contrast and vivid saturated colors (2x better feed performance)
- Clean, uncluttered — one hero element per slide
- Saveable designs (carousels get 22% more saves than single posts)
- Last slide: strong visual energy for CTA

## 2. CAROUSEL ANIMADO (Video Carousel)
**Format:** 9:16 vertical (1080x1920px) | 4-8 video slides | 3-5 sec per clip

**Engagement Advantage:**
- 2-3x more reach than static carousels (Instagram prioritizes video)
- Each slide counts as a separate "view," boosting watch time metrics
- Swipe-through rate is the key metric — IG re-serves unfinished carousels

**Motion Design Principles:**
- Slow, intentional camera movement: push-ins, gentle pans, parallax shifts
- One dominant motion per slide — no chaotic multi-directional movement
- Seamless transitions between slides (match-cut exit frame to entry frame)
- Subtle Ken Burns zoom on product/detail shots

**First-Frame as Thumbnail:**
- First frame must work as static thumbnail (most users see it paused)
- Bold text overlay on frame 1 with question or provocative statement
- Movement begins immediately — no static hold at start

**Audio/Music Sync:**
- Trending audio gets algorithmic boost
- Beat-sync slide transitions for polished feel
- Must work on mute (80%+ viewers) — text overlays carry the message

**Visual Consistency:**
- Uniform color grading/LUT across all clips
- Consistent text style, position, and animation direction
- Recurring branded element on every slide

**Viral Triggers:**
- Scroll-stopping first slide + curiosity gap demanding swipe
- Educational or transformation content outperforms pure aesthetics
- Seamless loop: last slide connects to first, triggering re-watches

## 3. POST (Single IG Image)
**Format:** 4:5 vertical (1080x1350px) | Full-bleed, no visible margins

**Scroll-Stop Fundamentals:**
- Single strong focal point — one subject, one message, one visual anchor
- High contrast between subject and background is #1 scroll-stop factor
- Pattern interruption: unexpected scale, color, or angle
- Human faces with direct eye contact increase dwell time 2-3x

**Color & Contrast:**
- Warm tones (amber, terracotta, coral) outperform cool tones for engagement
- Monochromatic or two-tone palettes read faster than multi-color
- Dark backgrounds with bright subjects ("light in tunnel" effect)
- Slightly desaturated rich tones feel premium — not neon

**Typography (graphic posts):**
- One typeface, two weights max (bold headline + light subtitle)
- Large type: minimum 1/3 of image height for primary word
- High-contrast text only — no medium-gray text
- Tight letter-spacing on uppercase headlines signals authority

**Save & Share Triggers:**
- Actionable value (tips, data, how-to) drives saves
- Emotionally resonant or identity-affirming content drives shares
- "Reference-worthy" design (checklists, frameworks, styled quotes)
- Bold number or statistic as visual anchor triggers bookmarks

**Professional Quality:**
- Consistent color grading (brand signature)
- Clean edges, precise alignment, intentional whitespace
- Subtle grain or texture adds premium tactile feel
- Logo: small, corner-placed, semi-transparent — never dominant

## 4. REEL (Video Vertical)
**Format:** 9:16 vertical (1080x1920px)

**Hook (First 1 Second):**
- Visually EXPLOSIVE opening — dramatic angle, extreme close-up, or unexpected reveal
- High contrast, vivid color, zero clutter
- Creates instant curiosity: "what is this?" or "I need to keep watching"

**Professional Video Quality:**
- Cinematic lighting: dramatic shadows, rim light, golden hour, studio lighting
- Depth of field: sharp subject, soft background (bokeh)
- Rich color grading: warm for lifestyle, cool for modern, vivid for energy
- Intentional negative space for text overlays
- Every frame should look like a premium brand ad

**Motion Design:**
- Suggest natural motion — camera zoom, rotation, parallax, reveal
- Depth layers (foreground, subject, background) for parallax animation
- Dynamic angles: overhead flat lay, 45-degree hero, extreme macro, sweeping wide

**Viral Characteristics:**
- Pattern interrupt: unexpected visual that breaks the scroll
- Visual satisfaction: oddly satisfying details, perfect symmetry, smooth transitions
- Emotional trigger: nostalgia, pride, wanderlust, aspiration
- Shareability: "I need to send this to someone" energy

## 5. PITCH B2B (PDF Landscape)
**Format:** A4 landscape (16:9) | 4-6 pages optimal

**Visual Design:**
- 60-70% whitespace per slide — cluttered = amateur
- 2 fonts max (serif heading, sans-serif body) + 3 brand colors + 1 accent
- Left-align body; center only headlines and CTAs
- Dark-on-light for readability; dark mode for premium feel

**Page Structure (4-6 pages):**
1. **Cover** — Logo, one-line value proposition, hero image. No paragraphs.
2. **Problem/Opportunity** — Client pain point or market gap. Their language.
3. **Solution/Product** — What you offer, shown visually (mockups, photos). Max 3 bullets.
4. **Social Proof** — Client logos, 1-2 stats, testimonial quote.
5. **Pricing/Partnership** — Clear tiers or terms. Tables over paragraphs.
6. **CTA** — Single clear next step, contact info, urgency element.

**Data & Imagery:**
- One key stat per slide, displayed large (48pt+)
- Real photography over stock; mockups in context build credibility
- Icons over bullet points
- Charts: max 2 data series, direct labels, no legends

**Trust & Conversion:**
- Consistent logo placement (same size, position) on every page
- Professional typography (proper kerning, 1.4-1.6x line height)
- Personalize: client name/logo on cover and throughout
- Lead with THEIR ROI, not your features
- Under 500 total words across all slides
- End with exactly ONE CTA
- PDF format, under 10MB

## 6. IMG → VIDEO
**Format:** 9:16 vertical (1080x1920px) | Single image animated

Inherits Reel best practices. The source image becomes the first frame,
and the system generates motion directives (pan, zoom, parallax) to animate it.

## 7. PERSONAJE HABLANDO (Character Speaking)
**Format:** 9:16 vertical (1080x1920px) | Character animation

Specialized flow — character description + dialogue generate a rich animation prompt
with: starting pose, animation physics, secondary motion, facial expressions,
timed actions synced to dialogue, and ending pose.
