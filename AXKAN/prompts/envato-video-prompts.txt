TIPS FOR BEST RESULTS
================================================================================

1. Upload your actual product image as the source
2. Keep prompts under 500 characters if possible
3. Avoid specific brand names and locations
4. Describe motion clearly (what moves, how fast, direction)
5. Specify camera movement explicitly
6. Include lighting description for consistency
7. Generate multiple versions and pick the best
8. Stitch 3-4 clips together for a 12-15 second video
