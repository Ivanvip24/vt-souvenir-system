Scroll-Driven Animation Prototype

Every pixel, frame by frame

148 images. One canvas. Controlled entirely by your scroll position. The same technique Apple uses on every product page.

Scroll to begin

The Technique

Pre-rendered image sequence

Each frame is a pre-rendered JPG from a 3D application. The scroll position determines which frame gets painted to the canvas.

The Engine

GSAP ScrollTrigger

GSAP maps scroll progress to a frame index. The canvas redraws at 60fps, creating the illusion of a 3D object responding to your scroll.

Performance

Why not video or WebGL?

Pre-rendered frames deliver photorealistic quality — ray-traced lighting, perfect reflections — without GPU-heavy real-time rendering. Works everywhere.

Production

Ready for your product

Replace these frames with your own 3D renders — Blender, Cinema 4D, KeyShot. Export 60–120 WebP frames and you're live.

Built to ship.
