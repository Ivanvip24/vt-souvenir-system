# FIFA World Cup 2026 - IP-SAFE Design Prompts
### 3D Renders | Colorful Editions
> These prompts celebrate the spirit of North American football culture using **national animals** (moose, jaguar, eagle), **flag colors**, **generic football terms**, and **cultural landmarks** -- all public domain elements. They do NOT use any FIFA intellectual property (logos, mascots, slogans, emblems, trophy images, or trademarked terms).

## PROMPT 1 - "Tri-Nation Football Fiesta" (3D Vinyl Toy Style)

```
FORMAT: 1:1 square composition
SUBJECT: Three North American national animals as premium 3D vinyl
collectible figures -- a Canadian moose, a Mexican jaguar, and an
American bald eagle -- each wearing LOUD, SATURATED streetwear in
their country's flag colors. Grouped around a neon-gold soccer ball
in a confident crew pose. The entire scene EXPLODES with color --
confetti, paint splashes, and graffiti-style energy.

COMPOSITION:
• The jaguar at center wearing a BRIGHT LIME GREEN hoodie with HOT
  PINK and WHITE Mexican serape-stripe accents, neon orange sneakers,
  one paw resting on the soccer ball (40% of frame)
• The moose to the left in a CHERRY RED bomber jacket with WHITE
  maple-leaf lining, ELECTRIC BLUE jeans, YELLOW high-tops, arms
  crossed confidently (30% of frame)
• The eagle to the right in a COBALT BLUE varsity jacket with CANDY
  RED sleeves, WHITE star details, GOLD chain, wings slightly spread
  (30% of frame)
• Neon-gold metallic soccer ball at their feet, radiating warm light
• MASSIVE CONFETTI EXPLOSION behind them -- hot pink, cyan, yellow,
  orange, lime green paper pieces filling the air
• Splashes of colored paint/ink behind each character matching their
  palette -- like they just burst through a color wall
• Small floating icons: maple leaves in RED, nopal cacti in BRIGHT
  GREEN, stars in ELECTRIC BLUE, marigold flowers in ORANGE

PROTAGONIST: Three stylized anthropomorphic vinyl toy figures with
chunky proportions, smooth matte skin, glossy eyes -- but dressed
in HYPEBEAST-level colorful streetwear that pops against the white
background like a color bomb went off

ELEMENTS:
• Canadian moose: cherry red + white + electric blue outfit
• Mexican jaguar: lime green + hot pink + neon orange outfit
• American eagle: cobalt blue + candy red + gold accents
• Neon-gold metallic soccer ball with warm glow
• HUGE confetti explosion in rainbow flag colors
• Paint/ink splash halos behind each character
• Floating cultural icons in saturated colors
• Tiny graffiti-style stars and swirls as accents
• Colored smoke wisps (pink, cyan, yellow) at their feet

Electric cyan, Marigold yellow, Metallic gold, Candy red, Pure white

bevel edges, 8% of frame. Below: "CANADA • MEXICO • USA" in bold

a FIESTA that jumped into a streetwear lookbook.

AND the confetti/paint explosions. The moose antlers break top-left,
the eagle's wing tips extend top-right, paint splashes and confetti
create organic irregular edges all around. NO rectangular frame.
BACKGROUND: Clean white / transparent -- making all the COLORS POP
even harder against the negative space
```

## IP COMPLIANCE NOTES

These prompts are **100% FIFA IP-safe** because they use:

| Element | Why It's Legal |
|---|---|
| Moose, Jaguar, Eagle | National animals -- cultural heritage, NOT FIFA mascots (Clutch/Maple/Zayu) |
| Flag colors | National flags are public domain |
| "Football 2026", "Summer of Football", "Futbol Para Todos" | Generic terms, none are FIFA trademarks |
| Country & city names | Public domain geographic names |
| Generic soccer ball | No FIFA branding |
| Landmarks (CN Tower, Angel, Statue of Liberty) | Public architectural references |
| Original character/emblem designs | Completely different style from FIFA's official assets |

### What was AVOIDED:
- Official Emblem (the "26" + trophy logo)
- Official Wordmarks ("FIFA World Cup", "World Cup", "Copa Mundial", "Mundial")
- Official Slogans ("We Are 26", "Somos 26")
- Official Mascots (Clutch, Maple, Zayu)
- Official Trophy image
- Official Typeface "FWC 26"
- Host City Logos
- Any FIFA branding or marks

> Reference: [FIFA World Cup 26 IP Guidelines, v2.0, June 2024](https://digitalhub.fifa.com/m/3567360896991b48/original/FIFA-World-Cup-26-IP-Guidelines.pdf)
