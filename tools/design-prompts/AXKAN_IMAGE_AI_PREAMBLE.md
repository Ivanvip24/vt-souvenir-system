# AXKAN Universal Style Preamble for Image Generation AI

## Paste this BEFORE every design prompt sent to any image AI (Gemini, DALL-E, Midjourney, etc.). Works for any destination, any style, any product.

---

## The Preamble (paste this as-is before every prompt):

```
You are generating a professional souvenir sticker illustration in the AXKAN style. Follow these rules exactly:

STYLE: Flat vector cartoon illustration. Bold black outlines (2-3pt) around every element. Solid vibrant color fills, no gradients. 10+ vivid fully saturated colors. Stylized cartoon landmarks and animals, never realistic or photographic. Everything looks like a professional die-cut souvenir sticker.

COMPOSITION: Extremely dense and packed. 15-25 overlapping elements with zero empty space inside the silhouette. Lush vegetation (trees, ferns, tropical leaves, flowers native to the region) fills every gap between elements. Everything overlaps and connects into ONE cohesive unified shape. Decoration density 8/10 minimum.

SILHOUETTE: Irregular organic die-cut sticker shape defined by the elements themselves. NO rectangles, squares, circles, frames, or decorative borders. Each side of the silhouette looks different. Thick dark outline (3-5pt dark brown or black) hugs the entire design as one unified shape.

BACKGROUND: Pure flat white. Nothing behind the design.

TEXT: The destination name must appear in large bold colorful letters (each letter a different vivid color) at 15-25% of the design height, typically at the bottom. Optionally the state or region in smaller text below. No other text allowed.

REFERENCE IMAGE: If a reference photo is attached, your illustration must be a stylized cartoon version of THAT SPECIFIC scene, matching its exact geography, structure, and proportions. Do not invent a different version.

REAL ELEMENTS ONLY: Every animal, plant, flower, cultural object, and decorative detail must actually exist in or be associated with this specific destination. No generic Mexican decoration.

OUTPUT: Generate the image only. No written response.
```

---

## After the preamble, add the specific design prompt using this formula:

```
Design a souvenir illustration of [DESTINATION NAME, STATE/COUNTRY]. [If reference photo attached: "Use the attached reference photo as your blueprint - match the specific geography, structure, and proportions of this exact scene, rendered in the AXKAN flat vector cartoon style."]. The centerpiece is [MAIN LANDMARK/ATTRACTION - specific name and brief visual description] occupying 50% of the composition, rendered as a stylized cartoon. Surrounding it: [3-4 LOCAL animals that actually live in this region, each doing a dynamic action], [3-4 LOCAL flora that actually grows in this specific region], [3-4 CULTURAL/REGIONAL elements unique to this specific destination and nowhere else], [2-3 additional decorative details from the area]. Dense local vegetation fills all gaps between elements. "[DESTINATION NAME]" in large bold colorful letters (each letter a different vivid color) at the bottom of the design, 20% height. "[STATE/REGION]" in smaller text below. Vibrant colors: [list 5-6 vivid color descriptions relevant to the destination]. Irregular organic silhouette shaped by [describe what defines the top, sides, and bottom edges]. White background. CREATE DESIGN
```

---

## How to fill in the formula for ANY destination:

**[MAIN LANDMARK/ATTRACTION]**: The single most iconic visual of the place. Describe what it looks like briefly. If you have a reference photo, say "Use the attached reference photo."

**[LOCAL animals]**: Research what animals ACTUALLY live there. Give each one an ACTION: "flying around," "perched on," "swimming through," "hovering near." Never static.

**[LOCAL flora]**: Research what plants ACTUALLY grow there. Pine forests? Tropical palms? Desert cacti? Jungle ferns? Use the REAL vegetation of the region.

**[CULTURAL/REGIONAL elements]**: What is THIS PLACE specifically famous for that no other place shares? Christmas ornaments? Surfing? Coffee? Pottery? Chocolate? Only elements that belong HERE.

**[DESTINATION NAME]**: The city/town/place name in big colorful letters. Each letter a different vivid color. This is the AXKAN signature.

**[Vibrant colors]**: Pull from the actual colors of the place. Turquoise water? Red clay? Green jungle? Golden sand? Pink bougainvillea? Always vivid and saturated.

**[Silhouette edges]**: What elements stick out to create the irregular shape? Trees at top, wings on left, waves at bottom, flowers on right, etc.
