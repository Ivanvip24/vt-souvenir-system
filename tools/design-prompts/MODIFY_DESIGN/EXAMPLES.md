## Example 1: Clothing Modification (Traditional Dress)

**Context:** Mushroom design with girl character in simple dress
**Goal:** Change to authentic traditional Huautla dress
**Word Count:** 140 words

### The Prompt:

```
The design features a girl in a simple dress beneath a mushroom. Transform her dress into the authentic Huautla traditional costume.

DRESS TRANSFORMATION:
Simple striped dress → Elaborate traditional dress with:
• White base fabric with bold horizontal stripe bands (hot pink alternating with turquoise)
• Large colorful floral embroidery squares on chest and lower skirt (red roses, orange/yellow flowers, green leaves)
• Ruffled pink short sleeves
• Square neckline with decorative trim
• White underskirt visible at bottom with small decorative motifs

Keep everything else exactly as is: mushroom, character's face, hair, braids, pose, background, text, ferns, sparkles.

Style: Match the existing illustrated art style - cartoon character with bold outlines and soft shading.

CREATE DESIGN
```

**Result:** ✅ Perfect - Dress changed exactly, everything else preserved

## Key Success Patterns

Across all successful prompts:

1. **Opening sentence** immediately states the transformation
2. **→ notation** makes changes crystal clear
3. **Action verbs** describe dynamic states ("jumping", "gathering", "tossing")
4. **Brief preservation** in one consolidated list
5. **150-200 word range** consistently
6. **Design brief tone** throughout
7. **"CREATE DESIGN"** ending

## What Didn't Work (For Reference)

**Failed Attempt - Word Count: 267**
- Too much detail overwhelmed the AI
- Heavy preservation language made AI too conservative
- Result: AI copied original or cropped image with no changes

**Success came from:**
- Cutting words by ~50%
- Leading with transformation
- Reducing preservation language
- Emphasizing what changes, not what stays
