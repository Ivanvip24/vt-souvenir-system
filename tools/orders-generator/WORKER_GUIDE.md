# Worker Guide - How to Upload Finished Product Photos

## Quick Instructions for Production Team

### 📸 After Finishing a Product

**When you complete a design, upload a photo of the actual finished product:**

1. **Open the order** in Notion (on tablet/phone/computer)
2. **Find the design** you just finished
3. **Click on the design** to open it
4. **Find "Finished Product Photo"** property
5. **Click "Finished Product Photo"**
6. **Click "Upload"**
7. **Take a photo** or **select from gallery**
8. **Done!** Photo uploaded

---

## Step-by-Step with Screenshots

### Step 1: Open the Order

- Your manager will share the Notion link
- Open it on your phone, tablet, or computer
- You'll see all the designs in gallery view (big image cards)

---

### Step 2: Find Your Design

- Scroll through the gallery
- Find the design you just finished producing
- Click on the card to open it

---

### Step 3: Upload Finished Photo

**You'll see TWO image fields:**

| Field Name | What It's For |
|-----------|---------------|
| **Reference Image** | The design reference (what it should look like) - DON'T TOUCH |
| **Finished Product Photo** | ← **UPLOAD HERE!** Your photo of the actual finished product |

**To upload:**
1. Click **"Finished Product Photo"**
2. Click **"Upload"** (blue button)
3. **On phone:** Choose "Take Photo" or "Photo Library"
4. **On computer:** Click "Upload" and select file
5. Photo will appear immediately!

---

### Step 4: Mark as Printed (Optional)

- After uploading the photo, check the **"Printed"** checkbox
- This helps track which designs are completed

---

## Why Two Image Fields?

**Reference Image:**
- Shows what the product SHOULD look like
- Uploaded automatically by the manager
- **Don't change this!** It's the design specification

**Finished Product Photo:**
- Shows what you ACTUALLY produced
- **This is where YOU upload photos!**
- Helps with quality control
- Shows the real result

---

## Tips for Good Photos

**Take clear photos:**
- ✅ Good lighting (natural light is best)
- ✅ Focus on the product (not blurry)
- ✅ Show the full design clearly
- ✅ Straight angle (not tilted)
- ❌ Avoid shadows or glare
- ❌ Don't cover parts of the design

**Multiple photos?**
- You can upload **multiple photos** to the same field!
- Just click "Upload" again to add more
- Useful for showing different angles

---

## What If I Made a Mistake?

**Uploaded wrong photo?**
1. Click "Finished Product Photo"
2. Hover over the wrong photo
3. Click the **trash icon** to delete
4. Upload the correct photo

**Uploaded to wrong design?**
1. Delete the photo from the wrong design
2. Open the correct design
3. Upload the photo there

---

## Mobile App (Easiest Way!)

**For production workers on the floor:**

1. **Install Notion app** on your phone/tablet
   - iOS: App Store
   - Android: Google Play Store

2. **Your manager will share the workspace**
   - You'll get an invite link
   - Click it to join

3. **Open the order** on your phone
   - Navigate to Orders → Your order
   - Tap a design card

4. **Take photo directly in Notion:**
   - Tap "Finished Product Photo"
   - Tap "Upload"
   - Tap "Take Photo"
   - Snap the finished product
   - Done! ✅

**Super fast!** Takes 10 seconds to upload a photo.

---

## Common Questions

**Q: Which field do I upload to?**
A: **"Finished Product Photo"** - this is YOUR field for actual product photos!

**Q: Can I upload multiple photos?**
A: Yes! Click "Upload" multiple times. Great for showing different angles.

**Q: What if I don't have a phone?**
A: Use the computer/tablet in the workshop. Same process!

**Q: Do I need to delete the Reference Image?**
A: **NO!** Never touch "Reference Image" - that's the design specification.

**Q: What format should photos be?**
A: Any format works! JPG, PNG, HEIC (iPhone), all work fine.

**Q: Can I upload from the gallery view?**
A: No, you need to **open the design card** first, then upload.

**Q: How do I know if it uploaded successfully?**
A: You'll see the photo appear immediately in the field!

## Need Help?

**Can't find the field?**
- Make sure you're inside a design page (not just viewing the gallery)
- Scroll down - the field is below the properties

**Can't upload?**
- Check your internet connection
- Make sure you have permission to edit
- Ask your manager to check your Notion access

**Photo not appearing?**
- Wait a few seconds for upload to complete
- Refresh the page
- Try again with a smaller photo

---

## Manager Note

**To grant workers access:**
1. Open the order page in Notion
2. Click "Share" (top right)
3. Add worker's email
4. Set permission to "Can edit"
5. Send invitation

Workers can now upload photos from any device!
